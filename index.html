A family business built on doing the job right

Farkas Flooring started as a straightforward proposition: source better materials than anyone nearby, hire people who genuinely care about installation craft, and treat every customer the way you'd want to be treated on a major home purchase. That philosophy hasn't changed.

We work directly with domestic and international mills to bring unfinished hardwood, prefinished engineered plank, and luxury vinyl tile to New Jersey homeowners and commercial clients. Because we control the supply chain end-to-end — from climate-controlled warehouse storage to your finished floor — we can stand confidently behind everything we sell and install.

Our office is in Hackensack, available by appointment only, so you get the full attention of a flooring specialist — not a floor salesperson working on commission. Learn about our hardwood installation, refinishing, and material selection services, or schedule a free consultation to get started.

Our services

From selecting the right material to the final coat of finish — we handle every phase of your project in-house.

Material Selection

Choosing the right wood is the foundation of any great floor. We go directly to the world's best suppliers — no middleman — so you get expert guidance and the best possible price on solid oak, engineered plank, LVT, and specialty formats like parquet, reclaimed, and rift-sawn.

Installation

The finest wood still fails with poor workmanship. Our installation team has decades of combined experience across every type of project — nail-down, glue-down, floating, custom inlays, stairs, and more. We do it right the first time so you never need a callback.

Refinishing

A well-maintained hardwood floor can last a lifetime. If your floors have lost their luster, our refinishing team will assess the condition, recommend the right treatment, and restore the surface to its original beauty — usually with next-day availability to fit your schedule.

Why Farkas Flooring

A lot of companies can drop off boxes. Here's what separates a finished floor you'll love from one that fails in two winters.

  • ✓
    Climate-controlled storage

    Your material is warehoused at stable temperature and humidity from the moment it arrives — no sitting on a job-site pallet in January.

  • ✓
    Moisture testing before every install

    We measure the subfloor and the wood before anyone pulls out a nail gun. Skipping this step is the #1 cause of cupping and gapping.

  • ✓
    Proper acclimation, every time

    Hardwood is a natural material that moves. We give it the time and conditions it needs to stabilize in your space before installation begins.

  • ✓
    Single point of accountability

    Same company supplies and installs your floor. When something needs attention, there's no finger-pointing between vendor and contractor.

  • ✓
    Decades of regional reputation

    We've been the floor under New Jersey homes and businesses long enough that our reputation does most of our advertising.
